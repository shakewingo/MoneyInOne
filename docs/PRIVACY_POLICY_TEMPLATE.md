## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how MoneyInOne ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Financial Information
MoneyInOne collects and stores the following financial information that you provide:
- Asset information (type, name, amount, currency, purchase details)
- Credit/liability information (type, amount, currency, due dates)
- Portfolio calculations and summaries

### Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- Device identifier (used to sync your data across devices)
- App usage analytics (for improving app performance)

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or personal contact information
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect biometric data
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:
- **App Functionality**: Storing and calculating your financial portfolio
- **Data Synchronization**: Syncing your data across your devices
- **Service Improvement**: Understanding how to improve the app
- **Technical Support**: Helping resolve any technical issues you may encounter

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
- Your device identifier is used to keep your data separate from other users

### Security Measures
- We use secure, encrypted connections for all data transmission
- Our servers are protected with modern security practices
- We regularly update our security measures to protect your data
