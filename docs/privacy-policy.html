Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

MoneyInOne ("the App") is developed by Yao Ying. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App connects to your self-hosted backend to retrieve asset and currency data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. The App does not collect, store, or share personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App may fetch market data from public financial APIs. No personally identifiable information is sent to these services.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Data Storage

Any data displayed in the App is fetched from your own server and is not stored on the device beyond the current session cache.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/shakewingo/MoneyInOne.
