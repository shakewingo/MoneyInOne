MoneyInOne is your personal wealth dashboard — track all your assets and liabilities across multiple currencies in one clean, modern app.

FEATURES
• Track stocks, crypto, gold, real estate, cash and more
• Real-time market prices for stocks, crypto and gold (XAU/USD)
• Multi-currency support with live exchange rates
• Net worth calculation across all your holdings
• Credits and liabilities tracking (loans, credit cards, mortgage)
• Clean dashboard with category breakdowns
• Works on iPhone and iPad

HOW IT WORKS
Add your assets and credits once. MoneyInOne fetches live market prices automatically and converts everything to your base currency, giving you an up-to-date picture of your net worth at a glance.

PRIVACY
Your data is stored privately and associated only with your device. No account or sign-in required.
